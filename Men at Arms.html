Welcome to the Men At Arms!

Rules:

  • There is a red pawn and a blue pawn. The Red Pawn Has to reach the top rwo of the board, while the blue pawn, the bottom row.
  • Press W A S or D to move every turn. The Color of the Banner on the sides tells you whose chance it is.
  • You can also use the mouse to place two walls per turn along the tiles, blocking movement there.
  • Every Player starts with 20 Walls to place, so be careful. You cannot block a player off completely, you must leave at least one possible route for them.
  • Have fun!

Controls:

  • Use First Click to drag the map around.
  • Press A to toggle between Sniper and Infantry Squad Types.
  • Press Q to increase Squad Deployment Size and W to increase it.
  • Press Right Mouse Button to deply a Squad with current settings.
